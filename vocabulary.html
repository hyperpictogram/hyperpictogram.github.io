The Hyper Pictogram Vocabulary

This is the vocabulary for Hyper Pictogram.

Vocabulary URI: http://pictogram.tokyo/vocabulary

Class: http://pictogram.tokyo/vocabulary#Pictogram

The class of pictograms.

Here are properties of the class:

http://pictogram.tokyo/vocabulary#title
A title of a pictogram; a string literal; multilingualized with `lang` attributes.
http://pictogram.tokyo/vocabulary#shortDescription
A short desciption of a pictogram; a string literal; multilingualized with `lang` attributes.
http://pictogram.tokyo/vocabulary#longDescription
A long description of a pictogram; a string literal; multilingualized with `lang` attributes.
http://pictogram.tokyo/vocabulary#image
An image of a pictogram; SVG is recommended.
http://pictogram.tokyo/vocabulary#author
Author(s) of a pictogram.
http://pictogram.tokyo/vocabulary#source
A source of a pictogram.
http://pictogram.tokyo/vocabulary#license
A license of a pictogram.
